# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We are committed to providing a welcoming and professional environment for everyone. We expect all participants to:

- Be respectful and constructive in communication
- Focus on technical merit and collaboration
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Spam or off-topic content in issues and discussions

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to [info@initmax.com](mailto:info@initmax.com). All reports will be reviewed and addressed appropriately.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other communication channels.
